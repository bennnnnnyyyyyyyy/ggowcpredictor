# Design Context

## Users
Internal Gulf Global Outsourcing employees using the World Cup predictor from desktop at work and from phones on the move. They need to log in quickly, enter predictions, check results, and scan rankings without fighting the layout.

## Brand Personality
Energetic, sporty, and company-branded, with a compact utility feel rather than a decorative consumer app. The interface should feel confident and readable, with a little stadium atmosphere but no clutter.

## Aesthetic Direction
Dark sports-console styling with GGO green accents, strong typographic hierarchy, and crisp data cards. Mobile layouts should feel intentionally reflowed, not just scaled down.

## Design Principles
- Prioritize readability and touch targets on narrow screens.
- Preserve the strong GGO football identity while reducing visual crowding.
- Use progressive disclosure so tables and controls stay scannable on phones.
- Keep key actions obvious: predictions, filters, sync, and leaderboard.
- Prefer fluid layouts and stacked cards over dense desktop-style tables on mobile.
